## One thing worth knowing (encode path)

I first wired the compressor through `processBulkImageJob` →
`imagePipeline.compressImage`, but the prototype's generated `encoderMap` /
features-worker is **WebP-only** — AVIF/JXL are proven via direct bridge calls
(`bridge.avifEncode` / `bridge.jxlEncode`), not through `compressImage`. So the
helper encodes directly through the bridge per format. If you want the
single-image and bulk paths to share `compressImage` for all three formats,
widen the generated active encoder surface (sync generator + active set) to
include avif/jxl, then the helper can switch back to `processBulkImageJob`.
